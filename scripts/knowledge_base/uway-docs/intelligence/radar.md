# Global Regulatory Radar

## 2026 Q2 Focus: Stablecoins & VASP Licensing

### 🇭🇰 Hong Kong (SFC/HKMA)
- **Stablecoin Issuers:** HKMA has granted first batch of licenses (HSBC, Standard Chartered). Requirement: 1:1 reserve in HKD or high-liquid assets.
- **VATP Transition:** SFC deadline for non-licensed exchanges to exit HK market was May 31, 2025. Current focus is on **Shared Order Book** regulations.

### 🇪🇺 European Union (MiCA)
- **Transaction Limits:** 200m EUR daily limit for non-euro stablecoins is in effect.
- **CASP Licensing:** Shift from local registrations to unified MiCA licenses.

### 🇸🇬 Singapore (MAS)
- **Travel Rule:** Strict enforcement on transactions > 1500 SGD.
- **Custody Rules:** New mandates for segregation of retail assets.
