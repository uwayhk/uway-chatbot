# Business Process

## How We Work

At Uway, we've streamlined our engagement process to ensure you get the right AI + compliance solution efficiently. Here's what to expect when working with us.

---

## 📋 Step-by-Step Process

### 1️⃣ Initial Inquiry

**What happens:**
- You reach out via our contact form, email, or LinkedIn
- We schedule a 30-minute discovery call
- You share your use case, pain points, and goals

**What we need from you:**
- Brief description of your business (payment provider, fintech, crypto, etc.)
- Current compliance challenges
- Target jurisdictions (HK, Singapore, UAE, etc.)
- Timeline expectations

---

### 2️⃣ Solution Scoping

**What happens:**
- We analyze your requirements
- Map your needs to our AI + compliance capabilities
- Identify regulatory touchpoints (HKMA, SFC, MAS, etc.)

**Deliverables:**
- Preliminary solution architecture
- Recommended AI models and data sources
- Compliance framework alignment
- High-level implementation timeline

**Timeline:** 2-3 business days

---

### 3️⃣ Proposal & Quotation

**What you'll receive:**

#### 📄 Solution Proposal Document
- Executive summary
- Technical architecture overview
- Compliance mapping (which regulations we address)
- Implementation phases
- Success metrics

#### 💰 Quotation Breakdown

| Component | Description | Pricing Model |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| **AI Agent Setup** | RAG knowledge base, model fine-tuning, integration | One-time + monthly |
| **Compliance Content** | Regulatory research, KB curation, updates | Monthly subscription |
| **Integration** | API connections (Sumsub, payment rails, etc.) | One-time or hourly |
| **Support & Maintenance** | SLA tiers (Standard, Premium, Enterprise) | Monthly |
| **Custom Development** | bespoke features, additional jurisdictions | Project-based |

**Typical Engagement Tiers:**

| Tier | Investment | Best For |
|------|------------|----------|
| **Starter** | $5K - $15K setup + $2K/mo | Early-stage fintechs, single jurisdiction |
| **Growth** | $20K - $50K setup + $5K/mo | Scaling payment providers, multi-region |
| **Enterprise** | $50K+ setup + $10K+/mo | Licensed institutions, complex requirements |

*Note: Final pricing depends on scope, integrations, and compliance complexity.*

---

### 4️⃣ Technical Deep Dive

**What happens:**
- 60-90 minute session with our technical team
- API documentation review
- Security & data privacy discussion
- Integration architecture walkthrough

**Who should attend:**
- Your CTO / Technical Lead
- Compliance Officer
- Product Manager

**Outcomes:**
- Finalized technical specification
- Integration timeline confirmation
- Risk assessment completed

---

### 5️⃣ Contract & Onboarding

**What you'll sign:**
- Master Services Agreement (MSA)
- Statement of Work (SOW)
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) - if handling personal data
- NDA (if not already in place)

**Onboarding kickoff:**
- Project manager assigned
- Shared workspace setup (Slack, Notion, or your preference)
- Weekly sync schedule confirmed
- Access credentials exchanged

---

### 6️⃣ Implementation & Deployment

**Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)**
- Knowledge base setup
- AI agent configuration
- Initial compliance content loaded

**Phase 2: Integration (Week 3-4)**
- API connections (KYC providers, payment systems)
- Custom workflow development
- Internal testing

**Phase 3: UAT & Training (Week 5)**
- User acceptance testing
- Team training sessions
- Documentation handover

**Phase 4: Go-Live (Week 6)**
- Production deployment
- Monitoring setup
- Post-launch support

---

## 🤔 Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does the whole process take?

**Typical timeline:** 4-8 weeks from initial contact to go-live, depending on complexity.

| Engagement Type | Timeline |
|-----------------|----------|
| Standard AI Agent | 4-6 weeks |
| Multi-jurisdiction Compliance | 6-8 weeks |
| Enterprise Custom Build | 8-12 weeks |

---

### Can we start with a pilot?

Yes! We offer **4-week pilot programs** for qualified prospects:
- Limited scope (single use case or jurisdiction)
- Reduced investment ($5K - $10K)
- Full evaluation & ROI analysis
- Credit toward full engagement if you proceed

---

### What if our requirements change mid-project?

We use **agile methodology**:
- Bi-weekly sprint reviews
- Flexible scope adjustment
- Change request process for major modifications
- Transparent impact assessment (timeline & cost)

---

### Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, all engagements include:

| Support Tier | Response Time | Coverage |
|--------------|---------------|----------|
| **Standard** | 48 hours | Business hours (HKT) |
| **Premium** | 24 hours | Extended hours (HKT + SG) |
| **Enterprise** | 4 hours | 24/7 with dedicated Slack channel |

---

### How do you handle confidentiality?

- NDA signed before deep technical discussions
- Secure data handling (encryption at rest & in transit)
- No client data used for training without explicit consent
- Option for on-premise or private cloud deployment
