# About Uway

**Uway Limited - AI-Powered Financial Compliance Solutions**

## Company Overview

Uway Limited is a Hong Kong-based technology company founded in May 2025, specializing in AI-powered compliance solutions for the financial services industry.

### Mission

To bridge the gap between artificial intelligence and financial regulatory requirements, making compliance more efficient, accurate, and accessible.

### What We Do

- **AI Compliance Consulting**: Helping financial institutions implement AI solutions that meet regulatory requirements
- **Knowledge Resources**: Providing up-to-date guidance on KYC/AML, cross-border payments, and regulatory frameworks
- **Technology Integration**: Connecting businesses with leading compliance technology providers

### Key Focus Areas

1. **AI + Financial Compliance**: Exploring practical applications of AI in regulated environments
2. **KYC/AML Best Practices**: Industry-leading approaches to customer due diligence
3. **Cross-border Payments**: Navigating complex international payment regulations
4. **Regulatory Intelligence**: Tracking updates from HKMA, SFC, MAS, and other regulators
