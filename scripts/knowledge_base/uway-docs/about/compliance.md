# Compliance Commitment

**Our dedication to regulatory excellence and ethical AI practices.**

## Our Principles

### 1. Regulatory First

All our solutions and recommendations are grounded in official regulatory guidance from recognized authorities including:

- Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA)
- Securities and Futures Commission (SFC)
- Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS)
- Financial Action Task Force (FATF)

### 2. Transparency

We believe in transparent AI systems:

- Clear documentation of AI capabilities and limitations
- Explainable recommendations with cited sources
- No black-box decision making

### 3. Data Privacy

We respect and protect sensitive information:

- No storage of customer data without explicit consent
- Compliance with Hong Kong PDPO requirements
- Minimal data collection principles

### 4. Continuous Monitoring

Regulatory landscapes evolve constantly:

- Regular updates to our knowledge base
- Ongoing tracking of regulatory changes
- Prompt notification of material updates

## Quality Assurance

All content in our knowledge base undergoes:

1. **Source Verification**: Cross-referencing with official regulatory documents
2. **Expert Review**: Validation by compliance professionals
3. **Regular Updates**: Scheduled reviews and refreshes
4. **Feedback Integration**: Incorporating user feedback and corrections
