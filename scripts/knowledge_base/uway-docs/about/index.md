# About Uway

## Who We Are

Uway is an AI-first compliance technology company founded in 2025, specializing in intelligent solutions for payment providers, fintech startups, and financial institutions across Asia and beyond.

---

## 🎯 Mission

**Empowering financial innovation through intelligent compliance.**

We believe that regulatory compliance shouldn't slow down innovation. Our mission is to:

- **Democratize compliance expertise** — Make regulatory knowledge accessible to startups and enterprises alike
- **Accelerate time-to-market** — Reduce compliance review cycles from weeks to hours
- **Bridge AI + Regulation** — Translate complex regulatory requirements into actionable, AI-driven workflows
- **Enable global expansion** — Help fintechs navigate multi-jurisdiction compliance with confidence

---

## 🌟 Vision

**To become the trusted AI compliance partner for every fintech building the future of finance in Asia and beyond.**

By 2030, we envision:
- 500+ fintech companies powered by Uway's compliance intelligence
- 90% reduction in compliance research time for our clients
- Real-time regulatory updates across 10+ jurisdictions
- AI agents that don't just answer questions — but proactively identify compliance gaps

## 💎 Core Values

### 🔒 Integrity First

Compliance is in our DNA. We practice what we preach:
- Transparent pricing, no hidden fees

- Clear communication of regulatory risks

### 🚀 Innovation with Purpose

We don't build AI for AI's sake:

We succeed when you succeed:

### 🌏 Global Mindset, Local Expertise

## 📊 What We Do

### 🧠 AI Compliance Agents

Intelligent assistants trained on:
- Regulatory texts (HKMA, SFC, MAS, PBOC, etc.)
- Industry guidelines and circulars
- Enforcement actions and case studies
- Best practices from licensed institutions

**Use cases:**
- "What are the AML requirements for crypto payment providers in HK?"
- "Draft a compliance memo for our new cross-border product"
- "Compare HK and Singapore e-KYC regulations"

---

### 📚 Knowledge Base Curation

We build and maintain:
- Structured regulatory databases
- Jurisdiction-specific compliance guides
- Industry-specific playbooks (payments, crypto, lending, wealth tech)
