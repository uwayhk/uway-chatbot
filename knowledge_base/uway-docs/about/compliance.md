# Compliance Commitment

**Uway's dedication to regulatory excellence and ethical AI.**

## Our Commitment

At Uway, compliance is not just a requirement—it's core to our DNA. We are committed to:

### 1. Regulatory Adherence

- Full compliance with Hong Kong AMLO (Cap. 615)
- Alignment with HKMA and SFC guidelines
- MAS Notice 626 compliance for Singapore operations
- GDPR compliance for EU data subjects

### 2. Ethical AI Principles

Following the MAS FEAT Principles:

- **Fairness**: AI systems free from bias and discrimination
- **Ethics**: Responsible AI development and deployment
- **Accountability**: Clear governance and oversight
- **Transparency**: Explainable AI decisions

### 3. Data Protection

- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Access controls and audit logging
- Data minimization principles
- Secure cross-border transfer mechanisms

### 4. Continuous Monitoring

- Regular compliance audits
- Ongoing staff training
- Real-time regulatory update tracking
- Proactive risk assessment

## Certifications & Standards

We maintain compliance with:

- ISO 27001 (Information Security)
- SOC 2 Type II (Service Organization Control)
- PDPO (Hong Kong Personal Data Protection)

## Governance

**Compliance Committee:**
- Quarterly reviews of compliance posture
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning
- Regulatory change impact analysis

**Reporting:**
- Direct reporting line to board
- Whistleblower protection policy
- Incident response procedures
