# About Uway

**Uway Limited - AI-Powered Financial Compliance Solutions**

## Company Overview

**Uway Limited** is a Hong Kong-based technology company founded in May 2025, specializing in AI-driven compliance solutions for financial services.

### Mission

To bridge the gap between artificial intelligence capabilities and regulatory compliance requirements, enabling financial institutions to leverage AI while maintaining full regulatory compliance.

### Focus Areas

- **AI + Compliance**: Regulatory technology for AI-driven financial services
- **KYC/AML Automation**: Streamlined identity verification and monitoring
- **Cross-border Payments**: Compliance infrastructure for international transactions
- **Regulatory Intelligence**: Real-time updates on compliance requirements

### Jurisdictions

- **Hong Kong**: Primary operations, HKMA/SFC regulated environment
- **Singapore**: MAS framework compliance
- **International**: Multi-jurisdiction compliance mapping

## Technology Stack

- **AI/ML**: NVIDIA NIM, Vertex AI, OpenRouter
- **Compliance**: Sumsub integration, automated screening
- **Infrastructure**: GCP, AWS, Oracle Cloud
- **Development**: Modern web technologies, API-first architecture
