## 🏠 Work Culture

### Distributed by Design

- Team members across Hong Kong, Singapore, and mainland China
- Async-first communication (written docs > meetings)
- Flexible hours, results-oriented
- Regular in-person meetups (quarterly)

### Learning & Growth

- Budget for conferences and certifications
- Weekly knowledge sharing sessions
- Access to regulatory databases and AI tools
- Encouraged to publish and speak at industry events

### Transparency

- Open book management (financials shared with team)
- Regular all-hands updates
- Clear career progression frameworks
- Feedback culture (upward, downward, peer-to-peer)

## 🎯 What We Look For

### For Compliance Roles

- Deep understanding of financial regulations
- Experience in payments, crypto, or fintech
- Strong research and writing skills
- Curiosity about AI and technology
- Ability to translate complex regulations into clear guidance

### For Engineering Roles

- Strong foundation in ML/AI (especially LLMs)
- Production experience with Python, TypeScript, or Go

- Customer-first mindset
