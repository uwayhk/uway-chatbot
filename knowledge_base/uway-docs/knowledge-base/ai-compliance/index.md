# AI + Financial Compliance

**Bridging artificial intelligence with regulatory requirements in financial services.**

## Overview

The intersection of AI and financial compliance represents one of the most critical frontiers in modern fintech. As regulatory frameworks evolve to address AI-driven financial services, organizations must navigate complex requirements while leveraging AI capabilities.

## Key Topics

### 1. Regulatory Frameworks

- **Hong Kong**: HKMA Supervisory Policy Manual, SFC Guidelines on Online Distribution
- **Singapore**: MAS Technology Risk Management, FEAT Principles
- **EU**: AI Act, GDPR for financial data
- **US**: OCC Guidance on Third-Party Relationships

### 2. AI Use Cases in Compliance

- **KYC/AML Automation**: Document verification, risk scoring, ongoing monitoring
- **Transaction Monitoring**: Real-time anomaly detection, pattern recognition
- **Regulatory Reporting**: Automated report generation, data validation
- **Risk Assessment**: Credit scoring, fraud detection, portfolio analysis

### 3. Compliance Challenges

- **Explainability**: Regulatory requirement for AI decision transparency
- **Data Privacy**: Cross-border data transfer restrictions
- **Model Risk Management**: Validation, monitoring, documentation
- **Bias & Fairness**: Ensuring non-discriminatory AI outcomes
